10 Richest People Under 30 — #1's Net Worth Will Make Your Jaw Drop

TR
TopRankMay 27, 2026789K views

Some were born into wealth, others built empires from their dorm rooms. Here are the 10 wealthiest people under 30 in 2026 — and their stories might surprise you.

10. Emma Chen (29) — $1.2 Billion

Founder of AI education platform LearnFast. What started as a tutoring app during her Stanford PhD became a company valued at $8 billion. Chen still codes every day.

9. Alex and Max Turner (27) — $1.5 Billion (each)

Twin brothers who sold their gaming startup to Microsoft for $3.2 billion. Their battle royale game "Zero Point" had 100 million players within six months of launch.

8. Priya Sharma (28) — $1.8 Billion

India's youngest female billionaire. Her company, GreenForm, manufactures biodegradable packaging that replaced single-use plastics across 40 countries. Started the business from her family's garage.

7. Marcus Wei (26) — $2.1 Billion

Crypto prodigy turned legitimate fintech founder. After making his first million at 16 trading Bitcoin, he built WeiPay — now processing $50 billion in transactions annually across Southeast Asia.

6. Olivia Brooks (29) — $2.4 Billion

Heiress to the Brooks Media empire who quadrupled the family fortune by pivoting the company to AI-powered content creation tools. Critics called her unqualified. The stock price disagrees.

5. Jamal Harris (28) — $2.8 Billion

Former NFL prospect who blew out his knee and taught himself to code during recovery. His logistics AI company OptiRoute is now used by Amazon, Walmart, and every major shipping company.

1. Kai Zhang (24) — $7.3 Billion

The youngest self-made billionaire in history. His quantum computing company Q-Next achieved practical quantum supremacy in 2025, solving problems in seconds that would take traditional supercomputers millennia. Zhang is 24 years old. He still has a roommate.
