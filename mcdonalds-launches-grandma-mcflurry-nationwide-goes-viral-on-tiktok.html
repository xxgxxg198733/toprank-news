McDonald's Launches 'Grandma McFlurry' Nationwide, Goes Viral on TikTok

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 06, 2026 ⏱ 1 min read 134,005 views

McDonald's has officially launched its newest dessert, the "Grandma McFlurry," nationwide on June 4, 2026, and it's already breaking the internet. The limited-time treat features a creamy vanilla soft serve base mixed with pieces of warm apple pie, a swirl of salted caramel, and a generous sprinkle of buttery graham cracker crumble.

TikTok Fame Drives Frenzy

The Granda McFlurry first appeared in test markets in Ohio and Texas in late May, but it exploded in popularity after a viral TikTok video by food influencer @SnackSquad showed the dessert being made. The video amassed over 50 million views in just three days, with users calling it "the best McFlurry ever" and "a hug from your grandmother in a cup." McDonald's confirmed that the nationwide launch was expedited due to overwhelming demand.

Nutritional Info and Price

The Granda McFlurry is available in two sizes: a snack size (330 calories) and a regular size (520 calories). Prices vary by location but typically range from $3.49 to $4.49. Some fans have criticized the calorie count, but McDonald's marketing emphasizes it as an occasional indulgence. The chain also launched a special "Granda Meal" that pairs the McFlurry with a McChicken sandwich and small fries for $7.99.

Competitors React

The success of the Granda McFlurry has put pressure on competitors like Dairy Queen and Burger King, who are reportedly rushing to develop their own nostalgic dessert offerings. McDonald's CEO Chris Kempczinski said in a press release, "We're thrilled to bring a taste of home to our customers. The Granda McFlurry is our most successful limited-time offering in five years." The item is expected to be available through September or while supplies last.
