10 Most Anticipated Movies of 2026 — Every Single One Looks Like a Box Office Monster

TopRankMay 28, 2026328K views

2026 is shaping up to be one of the biggest years in cinema history. From superhero epics to daring original projects, these are the 10 films everyone will be talking about.

1. Avengers: Eternity War (December)

The culmination of Marvel's Multiverse Saga. Every surviving Avenger, every multiverse variant, every cameo you can imagine. Early test screenings reportedly left audiences "emotionally devastated."

2. Dune: Messiah (October)

Denis Villeneuve completes his Dune trilogy with the most challenging book in the series. Timothée Chalamet's Paul Atreides grapples with the consequences of his jihad. Darker and weirder than Part Two.

3. The Odyssey (July)

Christopher Nolan's take on Homer's epic, shot entirely on IMAX film across seven countries. The cast includes Matt Damon as Odysseus and Zendaya as Circe. Expect practical effects — Nolan reportedly built a full-scale Trojan horse.

4. Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse (June)

The trilogy concludes. After years of delays, the animation team promises this is worth the wait. Over 240 unique animation styles were created for the different dimensions.

5. The Wager (November)

Martin Scorsese reunites with Leonardo DiCaprio for this true story of shipwreck, mutiny, and survival in the 1740s. Based on David Grann's bestselling book.
