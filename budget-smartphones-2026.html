10 Best Budget Smartphones of 2026 — Flagship Features for Under $400

TR
TopRankMay 29, 2026521K views

The gap between flagship phones and budget options has never been narrower. In 2026, $400 buys you features that cost $1,000 just two years ago. After testing over 20 budget phones, here are our top 10 picks.

1. Google Pixel 8a — $399

The best camera on any phone under $500, period. Google's computational photography makes every shot look professional. Seven years of software updates means this phone will last until 2033.

2. Samsung Galaxy A56 — $379

Samsung's mid-range champion gets a 120Hz AMOLED display, 50MP main camera, and the longest software support in the budget segment. The plastic body is the only giveaway that this isn't a flagship.

— Advertisement —

3. OnePlus Nord 5 — $349

OxygenOS remains the cleanest Android experience outside of Google's own phones. The Nord 5 adds a flagship-grade vibration motor and stereo speakers that belong on a much more expensive device.

4. Nothing Phone (3a) — $329

The Glyph LED lights on the back are a gimmick, but they're a fun gimmick. More importantly, Nothing's software is clean, fast, and free of bloatware. The unique design turns heads everywhere.

5. Xiaomi Redmi Note 15 Pro — $299

The value champion. A 200MP camera, 5,500 mAh battery, and 120W charging for under $300 is remarkable. The MIUI software is the trade-off — it's powerful but cluttered with pre-installed apps.
