Top 10 Best Smartphones of 2026: Rankings and Reviews

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 05, 2026 ⏱ 2 min read 141,068 views

With the first half of 2026 nearly complete, we've tested dozens of smartphones to bring you our definitive ranking of the top 10 devices available now. From foldables to flagship killers, here are the best phones money can buy in June 2026.

1. Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra

The Galaxy S26 Ultra retains the crown with its 200MP camera, Snapdragon 8 Gen 4 chip, and stunning 6.9-inch Dynamic AMOLED display with 5,000 nits peak brightness. The integrated S Pen and 10,000mAh battery make it a productivity powerhouse. Starting at $1,399.

2. iPhone 18 Pro

Apple's latest flagship features the A20 Bionic chip, a periscope zoom lens with 10x optical zoom, and a 48MP main sensor. The titanium frame and USB-C 4.0 port are welcome upgrades. iOS 20's AI features, including real-time translation and on-device ChatGPT integration, set it apart. Starting at $1,199.

3. Google Pixel 11

Google's Pixel 11 continues its camera dominance with a 64MP main sensor and a new 'Magic Editor Pro' that can remove any object or person from photos. The Tensor G5 chip delivers smooth performance and 7 years of OS updates. Battery life is a standout at 2 days. Starting at $799.

— Advertisement —

4. OnePlus 14

OnePlus returns to form with the 14, offering a 6.7-inch 120Hz LTPO display, 100W fast charging, and a Hasselblad-tuned triple camera system. The Snapdragon 8 Gen 4 chip and 16GB RAM make it a performance beast for $899.

5. Xiaomi 16 Pro

Xiaomi's flagship features a 1-inch Sony IMX989 sensor, 120W charging, and a 6.8-inch AMOLED display. The Leica partnership delivers incredible color science. At $899, it's a strong value proposition.

6-10: The Rest

Rounding out the top 10: Oppo Find X8 Pro ($1,099), Sony Xperia 1 VI ($1,299), ASUS ROG Phone 9 ($999), Motorola Edge 50 Ultra ($799), and the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 7 ($1,899). Each excels in specific areas like gaming, photography, or productivity.

Our advice: If you want the best overall, get the S26 Ultra. For iOS fans, the iPhone 18 Pro is unbeatable. And for pure camera magic, the Pixel 11 is unmatched.
