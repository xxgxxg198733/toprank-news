AirPods Max vs Sony WH-1000XM6: Which Flagship Headphones Win?

TR
By TopRank Team · June 15, 2026 · 3 min read

I've been a headphones nerd for years. I've owned everything from $20 earbuds to $500 studio cans. So when Apple updated the AirPods Max in April 2026 — finally adding USB-C and a slightly lighter frame — and Sony followed up with the WH-1000XM6 in May, I knew I had to compare them head-to-head.

I bought both. I used them for a full week each: commuting, working, lounging, flights. Here's my honest take.

Build and Comfort: Apple's Still Got It

The AirPods Max are undeniably premium. The new model uses recycled aluminum and a softer mesh headband. They feel like a luxury item. The Sony XM6, on the other hand, is lighter — almost 30 grams lighter — and that matters when you wear them for hours. I found the Sonys more comfortable for long stretches. But the Apple's build quality is unquestionably better. The metal ear cups, the telescoping arms... it feels like it'll last a decade. The Sonys are mostly plastic. They creak a bit when you move your head.

Sound Quality: Closer Than You Think

Sony's XM6 has improved bass response compared to the XM5. It's punchy, deep, and well-controlled. Apple's sound signature is more neutral — flatter, more accurate. For pop and electronic, Sony wins easily. For acoustic, classical, or vocals, Apple takes it. I listened to Diana Krall's new album on both and the AirPods Max captured the warmth of her voice better. But for Dua Lipa's latest? Sony had me bobbing my head.

Noise Cancellation: Sony is Still King

This is where Sony dominates. The WH-1000XM6 has the best ANC I've ever experienced. I wore them on a subway and heard almost nothing. The AirPods Max are good — great, even — but they let in a bit more high-frequency noise. The Sony's transparency mode is also more natural. If you live in a noisy city, get the Sony. No contest.

Ecosystem and Features: Apple Locks You In

The AirPods Max are deeply integrated with Apple devices. Switching between iPhone, iPad, and Mac is seamless. Spatial Audio is impressive for movies. But if you use Android or Windows? You lose most of the magic. Sony works perfectly with everything. The app is decent, the EQ is customizable, and the battery lasts 40 hours vs Apple's 20. That's a huge difference. I forgot to charge the Sony twice and it still had juice. The Apple needed charging every other day.

Price and Value

The AirPods Max cost $549. The Sony XM6 is $399. For $150 less, you get better ANC, longer battery, and cross-platform compatibility. The only reason to get the Apple is if you're all-in on their ecosystem and you value build quality above all else. I'm not sure that's worth the premium.

Final Verdict

I'm going with the Sony WH-1000XM6 as my pick. It's not perfect — the plastic build bothers me, and the sound is slightly less refined — but for everyday use, it's the better value. The AirPods Max are for audiophiles and Apple fanatics. The Sony is for everyone else. If I had to spend my own money today, I'd buy the Sony. And I did.
