Top 10 Best Hidden Gems in Europe for Summer 2026

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 05, 2026 ⏱ 2 min read 112,216 views

Tired of crammed beaches and overpriced hotels in Barcelona or Santorini? For summer 2026, we've curated 10 under-the-radar destinations in Europe that offer stunning beauty, authentic experiences, and a fraction of the crowds. Here are our picks.

1. Ksamil, Albania

Forget the Greek islands. Ksamil, on Albania's Ionian Riviera, boasts turquoise waters, white sand beaches, and affordable prices. A beachfront room costs as little as €40 per night. The nearby Butrint archaeological site is a UNESCO World Heritage site worth exploring.

2. Lake Bohinj, Slovenia

While Lake Bled draws Instagram crowds, neighboring Lake Bohinj is equally stunning but far quieter. Crystal-clear waters, hiking trails through Triglav National Park, and charming villages like Stara Fužina make it perfect for nature lovers. Rent a kayak for €10 per hour.

3. Matera, Italy

Matera, the European Capital of Culture 2019, remains a hidden gem with its ancient cave dwellings (Sassi) carved into limestone cliffs. Stay in a cave hotel for a unique experience. The city is now connected by high-speed rail from Rome (3 hours).

4. Mostar, Bosnia and Herzegovina

Mostar's iconic Stari Most bridge is less crowded than Dubrovnik or Split. The Old Town is a UNESCO site, and day trips to Kravica Waterfalls or the nearby Blagaj Tekke are unforgettable. Bosnia's visa-free access for US, UK, and EU citizens makes it easy to visit.

5. Braga, Portugal

Porto's little sister, Braga, offers beautiful baroque churches, the stunning Bom Jesus do Monte sanctuary, and a thriving food scene. It's a 30-minute train ride from Porto but with far fewer tourists. Try the local 'Bacalhau à Braga' cod dish.

6-10: More Gems

Other hidden gems include: Kotor, Montenegro (fjord-like bay), Rovinj, Croatia (Venetian architecture, fewer crowds than Dubrovnik), Ghent, Belgium (medieval charm, beer culture), Faroe Islands (dramatic cliffs, puffins), and Lviv, Ukraine (coffee culture, affordable luxury). Each offers unique experiences without the tourist hordes.

Pro tip: Book flights early. Airlines like Ryanair and WizzAir now offer direct routes to many of these destinations from major European hubs.
