Top 10 Most Instagrammed Travel Destinations This Summer 2026 Revealed

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 03, 2026 ⏱ 1 min read 464,312 views

Instagram released its annual list of the top 10 most hashtagged travel destinations for summer 2026 on June 2, based on billions of posts. The ranking reflects a shift toward off-the-beaten-path locations and sustainable tourism.

Number 1: Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon, Iceland

This majestic canyon in southeast Iceland takes the top spot, with posts up 300% year-over-year. Known for its lush green walls and waterfall, it gained fame after a viral drone video last month. Travelers are flocking to hike its rim, though officials urge caution to protect the fragile ecosystem.

— Advertisement —

Rising Stars: Nosy Be, Madagascar and Svalbard, Norway

Nosy Be, a tropical island off Madagascar, ranks third for its pristine beaches and lemur encounters. Svalbard, in the Arctic, debuts at fifth, driven by northern lights and polar bear tours. Both destinations emphasize eco-tourism, with local guides promoting wildlife conservation.

Classic Favorites Slip: Paris and Bali Drop

Paris fell from first to fourth due to overtourism backlash, while Bali dropped to sixth. Instead, travelers favor places like Georgia's Caucasus Mountains (second) and Mexico's Bacalar Lagoon (seventh). The data shows a clear trend: authenticity and nature beat crowded landmarks in 2026.
