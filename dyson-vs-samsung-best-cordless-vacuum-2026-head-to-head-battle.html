Dyson vs Samsung: Best Cordless Vacuum 2026 Head-to-Head Battle

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 05, 2026 ⏱ 1 min read 116,010 views

The cordless vacuum market in 2026 is dominated by two giants: Dyson and Samsung. With both companies releasing their flagship models earlier this year, consumers are torn. We tested the Dyson Gen5Detect ($799) and the Samsung Bespoke Jet AI ($749) across seven critical categories. Here’s the full head-to-head.

Round 1: Suction Power

The Dyson Gen5Detect boasts 262 air watts on max mode, while the Samsung Bespoke Jet AI outputs 280. Samsung’s HexaJet motor, spinning at 160,000 rpm, ekes out a win. Winner: Samsung.

Round 2: Battery Life

Dyson claims 60 minutes on eco mode; we got 58. Samsung offers two swappable batteries (48 minutes each) for a total of 96 minutes. For whole-home cleaning, Samsung wins. Winner: Samsung.

Round 3: Weight and Maneuverability

The Dyson weighs 5.9 lbs in handheld mode, Samsung 6.3 lbs. Dyson’s new digital motorbar brush swivels 180°, making it easier under furniture. Winner: Dyson.

Round 4: Filtration

Both have HEPA‑13 filters, but Dyson’s sealed system captures 99.99% of particles down to 0.3 microns. Samsung’s exhaust filter is also HEPA‑13, but its bin is slightly less sealed. Winner: Dyson.

Round 5: Dustbin Capacity

Dyson’s bin is 0.77L; Samsung’s is 0.8L. Both are easy to empty with a single button. Winner: Tie.

Round 6: Attachments and Storage

Samsung includes seven attachments (including a pet tool and a slim crevice tool) and a wall-mounted charging station with self-cleaning brush. Dyson has five attachments, but no self-cleaning. Winner: Samsung.

Round 7: Price and Warranty

Dyson: $799, 2‑year warranty. Samsung: $749, 5‑year warranty on motor. Winner: Samsung.

Final score: Samsung 5, Dyson 2. The Samsung Bespoke Jet AI offers better value for most households, but Dyson remains best for lightweight portability.
