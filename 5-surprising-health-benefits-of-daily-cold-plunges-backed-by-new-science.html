5 Surprising Health Benefits of Daily Cold Plunges Backed by New Science

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 03, 2026 ⏱ 1 min read 58,306 views

Cold plunges have surged in popularity, and a comprehensive study published on June 2, 2026, in the journal Nature Medicine provides robust evidence for their health benefits. Researchers at Stanford University tracked 1,000 participants over two years, measuring physiological and psychological changes.

Benefit 1: Enhanced Immune Response

Participants who took 3-minute cold plunges at 10°C daily had 30% fewer respiratory infections. The cold stress triggers a spike in white blood cells, particularly natural killer cells, which fight viruses. The effect lasted up to 24 hours post-immersion, suggesting consistent practice is key.

Benefit 2: Improved Mood and Reduced Anxiety

Cold exposure stimulates dopamine release by 250%, according to brain scans, leading to sustained mood elevation. Participants reported a 40% reduction in anxiety scores after 4 weeks. This aligns with anecdotal 'cold shock' therapy used by athletes like Wim Hof.

Benefit 3: Metabolic Boost and Fat Loss

The study found that cold plunges increased metabolic rate by 15% for 2 hours post-exposure, due to activation of brown fat. Over 6 months, participants lost an average of 3.5kg without diet changes. However, experts caution against overuse, urging gradual adaptation to avoid hypothermia.
