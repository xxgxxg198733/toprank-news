Score 10: New Zealand vs England 1st Test Live Updates

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 05, 2026 ⏱ 1 min read 442,186 views

Day 1 of the first Test between New Zealand and England is underway at Hagley Oval, Christchurch, with England winning the toss and electing to bat first. The match marks the beginning of a three-Test series, the first time England has toured New Zealand since their historic 2023 visit.

England's Innings

Opening pair Ben Duckett and Zak Crawley have put on a steady 56-run partnership before Duckett was caught behind off Tim Southee for 34. Captain Ben Stokes walked in at No. 3, signaling an aggressive intent. At lunch, England is 87/1 with Crawley on 41* and Stokes on 12*.

New Zealand's Bowling Attack

New Zealand's pace trio of Tim Southee, Matt Henry, and debutant Will O'Rourke have bowled disciplined lines, but the pitch at Hagley Oval is offering little assistance. Spinner Mitchell Santner has been introduced early in a bid to break the partnership. The Black Caps will be hoping for early wickets after tea to stay competitive.

Series Context

England, under Stokes and coach Brendon McCullum, have won 13 of their last 17 Tests with their 'Bazball' approach. New Zealand, meanwhile, are rebuilding after the retirements of Kane Williamson and Trent Boult. The series is crucial for both teams' World Test Championship standings.

Stay tuned for live updates as the action unfolds. England will look to post a big first-innings total, while New Zealand seeks early breakthroughs.
