10 'Healthy' Habits That Are Secretly Damaging Your Body

TR
TopRankMay 31, 2026453K views

We all try to make healthy choices. But some of the most common "healthy" habits might actually be working against you. Here are 10 wellness practices that experts say you should reconsider.

1. Drinking Fruit Juice Instead of Soda

Fruit juice sounds healthier than soda, but nutritionally they're remarkably similar. Both are high in sugar with minimal fiber. A 12-ounce glass of orange juice contains about 34 grams of sugar — nearly the same as a can of Coke. Eat the whole fruit instead.

2. Hitting 10,000 Steps Religiously

The 10,000-step goal was invented by a Japanese pedometer company in the 1960s, not by medical researchers. Studies show health benefits plateau at around 7,500 steps per day. Obsessing over a specific number can lead to overuse injuries.

3. Taking Multivitamins 'Just in Case'

Multiple large-scale studies have found that multivitamins provide little to no health benefit for people eating a balanced diet. Some fat-soluble vitamins can build up to toxic levels. You're literally paying for expensive urine.

4. Avoiding All Carbs

The anti-carb movement threw the baby out with the bathwater. Complex carbohydrates from whole grains, legumes, and vegetables are essential for energy, brain function, and gut health. The Mediterranean diet — consistently ranked the healthiest — includes plenty of carbs.

5. Using Hand Sanitizer Obsessively

Alcohol-based sanitizers kill both harmful and beneficial bacteria on your skin. Overuse disrupts your skin microbiome and can lead to dermatitis. Soap and water is almost always the better choice.

6. Sleeping With Your Phone on Night Mode

Night mode reduces blue light, which is good. But using your phone in bed still stimulates your brain, delays melatonin production, and trains your brain to associate your bed with activity rather than sleep. Leave the phone in another room.
