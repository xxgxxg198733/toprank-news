Top 10 Best New Summer Blockbusters of 2026 (Ranked by Critics)

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 04, 2026 ⏱ 2 min read 327,855 views

Summer 2026 is shaping up to be a record-breaking season at the box office, with a slate of highly anticipated sequels, fresh IPs, and independent darlings. We've crunched the numbers from Rotten Tomatoes and Metacritic to bring you the definitive ranking of the 10 best new summer blockbusters. All scores are as of June 3, 2026.

The Top 5

At number 1 is Avengers: Secret Wars (98% RT, 89 Metacritic), the epic conclusion to the Multiverse Saga, directed by the Russo Brothers. Critics praise its emotional depth and stunning visual effects. Number 2 is Zootopia 2 (96% RT, 86 Metacritic), which expands the animated world with a mystery plot that is 'both hilarious and heartwarming.' Number 3 is Dune: Messiah (95% RT, 84 Metacritic), Denis Villeneuve's cerebral sci-fi sequel that is being called 'a masterpiece of scale and intimacy.'

Middle of the Pack

Number 4 is Mufasa: The Lion King (91% RT, 80 Metacritic), a prequel that uses stunning photorealism to tell the story of Simba's father. Number 5 is Deadpool & Wolverine (90% RT, 78 Metacritic), which fans are calling 'the funniest Marvel movie yet' despite its R-rating. Number 6 goes to Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga (89% RT, 76 Metacritic), a prequel starring Anya Taylor-Joy that 'expands the wasteland lore brilliantly.' Number 7 is Mission: Impossible – The Final Reckoning (88% RT, 75 Metacritic), Tom Cruise's seventh outing as Ethan Hunt, delivering 'the most insane stunts ever filmed.'

The Bottom of the List

Number 8 is Inside Out 2 (87% RT, 74 Metacritic), Pixar's sequel that explores teenage emotions with typical cleverness. Number 9 is Alien: Romulus (85% RT, 72 Metacritic), a sci-fi horror return to form that 'recaptures the terror of the original.' Rounding out the top 10 is The Fall Guy (84% RT, 71 Metacritic), Ryan Gosling and Emily Blunt's action-comedy that is 'pure, unadulterated fun.' All of these films are currently in theaters or streaming, making for a fantastic summer at the movies.
