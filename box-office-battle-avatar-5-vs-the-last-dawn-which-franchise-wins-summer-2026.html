Box Office Battle: 'Avatar 5' vs. 'The Last Dawn' – Which Franchise Wins Summer 2026?

TR
TopRank Editorial Team June 03, 2026 ⏱ 1 min read 32,526 views

Summer 2026 is shaping up as a titanic box office duel between James Cameron's 'Avatar 5' and Christopher Nolan's new sci-fi epic 'The Last Dawn.' Both films opened on Memorial Day weekend, May 29, and have dominated headlines ever since.

Avatar 5: The Visual Spectacle Continues

'Avatar 5: The Spirit of Eywa' earned $340 million globally in its first 5 days, the second-highest opening ever. Critics praise its groundbreaking 3D underwater sequences and emotional depth, but some note franchise fatigue. The film's budget of $500 million makes it the most expensive movie ever, relying on record-breaking ticket sales.

— Advertisement —

The Last Dawn: Nolan's Cerebral Hit

Nolan's 'The Last Dawn,' an original story about time dilation and climate collapse, surprised analysts with $280 million in its opening weekend. Audiences rave about its intricate plot and stunning IMAX visuals. With a grade A+ CinemaScore, it has strong word-of-mouth, threatening 'Avatar 5's' longevity.

The Winner? Both, says Industry

Box office experts say both films are boosting the industry, which had a slow spring. 'Avatar 5' leads in raw earnings, but 'The Last Dawn' has a higher per-screen average. Social media buzz shows 'The Last Dawn' trending for its philosophical themes, while 'Avatar 5' dominates for visual effects. The ultimate winner may be the theatrical experience itself.
